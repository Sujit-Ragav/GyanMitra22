About Gyan Mitra

GYAN MITRA is a Two Days National Level Techno Symposium, which is a colloquium of 8 Technical domains including Bio-Tech, Civil, Computer Science, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, MCA and Maths & Science.


It is hosted by 25 Student’s Professional Societies like The Institution of Engineers (India) of all branches, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE), International Association of Civil Engineering Students (IACES). CSI Students Chapter, Society of Automobile Engineer, Biotech Association, MCA Association, Science Forum and Apti-Riders Club. It also comprises of Microsoft Campus Club, Google Students Club, ASME, Renewable Energy Club, Robotics Club and Maths Club. The symposium is organized continuously from 2015 onwards which aims to enhance the technical and leadership skills of the students.


GYAN MITRA ’23 is a 7th year in this series, which has 27 Workshops on Emerging Technologies, Paper Presentations in 14 Parallel Sessions and 116 Technical Events. The symposium provides avenue for students to present their latest innovations, findings, and trends in advanced technology, tools, techniques and strategies relevant to the Engineering fraternity. We delighted to invite all intellectually curious minds to participate in this GYAN MITRA '23.
